SCMission contest
  • 2 Comments
  • January 31, 2020
  • by Tran Tu Anh
  • USV, QGC, Web Server, Robotics

SCMission is an annual national contest, held by Logistics Studying club. Participants will have to solve business cases, analysis and processing data in a group of 4 through 4 rounds: multiple choices for round 1, case studies and data analysis given by the organizer in round 2, solving practical business cases given by sponsor in 3rd round and present that idea in final round.

The contest is organized entirely by students and sponsored by several big brands such as Coca Cola, ITL, Decathlon, Shopee, Cel Consulting, VNExpress, etc.

As a core organizing team member, speciaized in Marketing and Media department, I was responsible for:

+ Together with teammates make plan to marketing and create concept

+ Design publications and visuals related (banner, poster, standee, backdrop, flyers, etc.)

+ Working with content teams to create posts on fanpage updating informations about the contest and Logistics knowledge
